About Berry Catch üçì

Berry Catch is a pastel inspired arcade game where players control a basket to catch falling berries before they hit the ground. The goal is simple but exciting. Catch as many as possible while avoiding too many misses.

The game is inspired by classic catching arcade games and soft aesthetic browser mini games. We wanted to combine cute visuals, glowing effects, and relaxing colors with competitive gameplay.
